Babai Mart Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Babai Mart respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it and your rights while using our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Delivery Address
  • Current Location (Only with your permission)
  • Order History
  • Profile Photo (if uploaded)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create your account.
  • To process and deliver orders.
  • To identify your delivery location.
  • To send order updates and notifications.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To prevent fraud and misuse.

3. Permissions Used

  • Location – To identify delivery address and nearby service.
  • Camera – To upload profile or product images.
  • Photos & Storage – To upload images from your device.
  • Notifications – To send order updates and important alerts.

4. Data Sharing

Babai Mart does not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only when required to complete your order, provide customer support or comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Security

We use secure technologies and reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. Although no method is 100% secure, we continuously work to protect your data.

6. Account Deletion

Users can permanently delete their account directly from the Babai Mart app.

Open the app and go to:

Profile → Delete Account

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Delivery Address
  • Profile Photo
  • Order History (where applicable)

Some information may be retained for a limited period if required by applicable law, tax regulations or fraud prevention.

7. Children's Privacy

Babai Mart is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with the updated revision date.
